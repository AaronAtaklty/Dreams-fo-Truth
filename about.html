Our Origin

Dreams of Truth was founded in 2024 after a historic moment in human history—a period where artificial intelligence, rapid technological change, and political divides left many people disillusioned. A group of visionaries asked: What if humanity could dream again while still holding fast to truth?

Founders

Dr. Elena Marquez

AI ethicist and neuroscientist who discovered the neurological link between dreams and truth-seeking behavior.

Samir Okafor

Human rights advocate, inspired by his experiences in rebuilding post-conflict nations through visionary leadership.

Leah Sung

A poet and philosopher whose writings emphasize the harmony of imagination and honesty in society.

Our Mission

We believe that by cultivating meaningful dreams and aligning them with objective truth, individuals and societies can overcome fear, corruption, and stagnation. Our mission is to inspire a global movement of “Dreamers of Truth”—leaders, artists, scientists, and everyday people who dare to imagine and act with integrity.
